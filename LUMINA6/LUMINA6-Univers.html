📜 LU●MINA6 Lore

🌌 1. INTRODUCTION: The Birth of LU●MINA6

LU●MINA6 is a six-member male K-pop group born from light, resistance, and multilingual unity. They represent six emotional cores — called LUMACORES — each symbolizing a different force: light, pride, truth, fire, reflection, and gravity.

Together, they form the LUNAVERSE — a mythic universe where identity is power and queerness is a star system.
